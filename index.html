Fast Multiplication

Multiplying by 11 is the same as multiplying by 10 and adding once more. Multiplying by 101: multiply by 100 and add. Multiplying by 99: multiply by 100 and subtract. Each of these can be done in a single line — just shift the digits and stack. Try it: type any number and pick a multiplier.
